Introduction

I will highlight key points of the run starting with the Skeleton tutorial room

For further information regarding the first two rooms of the game, visit the tutorial section

Ecclesia - Skeleton Tutorial Room

As mentioned in the Skeleton Room Info section, we want to force the Skeletons to spawn in front of Shanoa

In NMC, we pick up a Heart Max Up in the Monastery, so we can use all of our Unions here

Get a single jump slash on the first Skeleton, then do a grounded Union close to it, hitting the second one as well

If done right, 2 more will spawn infront of Shanoa, and you repeat the sequence

The key here is how close you do the Union to the Skeletons and remaining grounded as you do

If done in the air, the Union will miss or cancel early, and if done far away, less of it will hit

Just make sure you're not too close to the first Skeleton or you risk triggering a spawn behind you

Monastery

Confedere Cancel out of the Skeleton Room and slide off the ledges/jump to cancel you fall end lag to exit Ecclesia

Confedere Cancel through the entrance of the Monastery, near the end of the outside area you'll run out of MP so use other forms of BDC.

Monastery Skeleton Room

If the first Skeleton throws a bone, jump above it then damage boost off the bone to get knocked past it

BDC to the next Skeleton and damage boost off of it then jump over the next

Finally, damage boost off the next Skeleton's bone or body to get through the end of the room

It is RNG if they throw a bone at the right time or not, so be prepared to alternate between what you're boosting off
