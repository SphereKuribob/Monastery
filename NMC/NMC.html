The Most Accessible Category and The Gateway to Ecclesia's Evolution

Introduction

I will highlight key points of the run starting with the Skeleton tutorial room

This won't be a room by room guide since watching a run will probably better explain the movement in each room

For further information regarding the first two rooms of the game, visit the tutorial section

Ecclesia - Skeleton Tutorial Room

As mentioned in the Skeleton Room Info section, we want to force the Skeletons to spawn in front of Shanoa

In NMC, we pick up a Heart Max Up in the Monastery, so we can use all of our Unions here

Get a single jump slash on the first Skeleton, then do a grounded Union close to it, hitting the second one as well

If done right, 2 more will spawn infront of Shanoa, and you repeat the sequence

The key here is how close you do the Union to the Skeletons and remaining grounded as you do

If done in the air, the Union will miss or cancel early, and if done far away, less of it will hit

Just make sure you're not too close to the first Skeleton or you risk triggering a spawn behind you

Monastery

Confedere Cancel out of the Skeleton Room and slide off the ledges/jump to cancel your fall end lag to exit Ecclesia

Confedere Cancel through the entrance of the Monastery, near the end of the outside area you'll run out of MP so use other forms of BDC.

Monastery Skeleton Room

If the first Skeleton throws a bone, jump above it then damage boost off the bone to get knocked past it

BDC to the next Skeleton and damage boost off of it then jump over the next

Finally, damage boost off the next Skeleton's bone or body to get through the end of the room

It is RNG if they throw a bone at the right time or not, so be prepared to alternate between what you're boosting off

Magical Ticket

Make sure to open Brown Chests for a Magical Ticket

Always go for the one in the Vertical Room since getting it from this Chest saves time on this entire segment

If you see a Green Chest go for it as well, as it can contain a Heart Refresh, which saves time later in the run

According to hellagels, Brown Chests have a 22.45% chance to drop a Ticket

Across the usual 3 Chests we grab, this equates to a 53.4% chance roughly to at least get 1

Magnes

Proceed through until the Magnes room as the run does

If any Skeleton Jumps as you approach or slide through it, get hit by them for a good damage boost

After absorbing Magnes, it's faster to jump to cling on to the pin above you, then launch Shanoa from there

For the second launch to the exit, I like to slightly pull back down and left before launching Shanoa to get to the room transition

Magnes Dash through the zombie room, damage boosting off zombies as you proceed

Rest of Monastery

We want to pick up the Heart Max Up from the secret room accessed by breaking the top left wall in the Gold Chest room

Every enemy in the Heart Max Up Room has an RNG spawn, so do your best to damage boost off the Ghosts and Zombies

Continue right through the area. In the next Vertical Room, if you do not have a Ticket or if it's a Green Chest, go for the chest

I like to jump slash the first two bats then Slide to the chest, be mindful to not backdash into the bat as you leave the section

As you ascend, grab the Money Bag. The bat shouldn't hit you if you backdash jump over it and land on the bag

Continue left, using the Zombies and Skeletons to damage boost, and grab the Chest on the way

Note: if you're getting really unlucky, you can instead quickly go right for another chest, but this is very slow

Arthroverta

We're going to skip the MP Max Up before the boss

At the start of the fight I like to jump slash into Arthro

After, if you take a look at the background you want to position yourself infront of Arthro at about the center of that second pillar

From this position, when you do a Grounded Union, it will hit 4 times

The strategy is to Union -> Couple Normal Hits -> Repeat Until 0 Hearts before the first roll

Afterwards, spam Confedere until Arthro dies. I like to stand a bit away when landing normal hits, to give room for a slide under the Green Web attack

Also, if you dodge away, it's good practice to close the distance with a jump slash in

Important: when she does her Claw Slam, you can hit her claws too

Below is the general idea for the fight:

If you are struggling to get all your Unions in before the first roll, it's completely fine to space them out after

Just be mindful to positon yourself close enough to get 4 Union hits in and not take damage

Leaving Monastery

After the fight, Magnetize on the left pin and hold right. The boss orb will spawn on you

Hold left after you grab it, release -> land on the right ledge -> immediately slide into the next room

As pointed out to me by LonelySR, sliding into the Albus Cutscene, then skipping the scene stops Shanoa from walking away from the left exit

Do the Instant Flings while exiting left through the boss room

Go down to the save -> save the game -> then suspend the game to go back to the title screen

The next section is where this run majorly diverges from Glitchless

Initiating Ticket Glitch

After the suspend, go to boss rush and select Course 1

Important: Suspending is faster in the Japanese version as it does not ask for English or French, this saves less than 5 seconds through the whole run

Once in Course 1, perform the Ticket Glitch immediately

This will take you to the Village where you want to Save the Game

Use the Dominus Collection Menu to reset the game then load the Main Save

We have just skipped leaving the Monastery normally AND Shanoa is now Level 50 for the rest of the run

Refresh to 30 Hearts, save the Villager, and go to Ruvas Forest

Rusalka

I do a jump slash in then a single grounded union

Goliath

Fun fight here, and all credit goes to Smith for these strats

Backdash in, turn around and do Double Hatred into Puff Union, avoid an attack if Goliath does one, then do the same attack sequence again

This is enough damage to make Goliath crouch, allowing you to also crouch at his toes and start spamming Confedere (found by Halgorithm)

Use your High Mind Up after a couple attacks since you'll be out of MP

Keep attacking, when Goliath gets up, stand and use a Confedere Union, make sure his hand is fully extended so it doesn't hit you

Do a couple more attacks, and as he's crouching, use your final union

Tristis Pass

Oblivion Ridge

Damage Boost through the first room. You may have to watch your MP here

Take the save heal, dash to the boss room

The Gravedorcus Quick Kill

There's multiple ways to do this, but I'll outline how I do it

Briefly wait to trigger Grave's Jump Attack, then slide off the right

Do a Double Hatred Cast when he's above you, and wait for him to surface to the right

As he surfaces, use a Puff Union -> Double Hatred Cast -> Backdash

You want to do this quick since being this close to him will trigger his Poison Breath if you don't get away fast enough

During the backdash, Go to Equipment Menu -> Macir Sleeve -> Replace 1 Macir with Vol Grando -> Exit Menu

Quickly, Grando + Macir Union Cancel until 0 Hearts, crouch infront of him as he digs, then Double Hatred Cast

Other runners do this slightly different, so try them out and do what works for you

If Gravedorcus surfaces left of you after the start, you can do the same thing but you need to adjust your positioning so that the Union doesn't ghost hit

If you fail the end of this, it's not a big deal, he should be 2-4 regular hits away from death

Exiting Oblivion Ridge

You can Dive Kick over the plant or dash through it, then continue left and Damage Boost through

Enter the room -> skip the cutscenes -> Magical Ticket out

Head to Ecclesia scale up as you did at the start of the game and trigger the Barlowe Cutscene by entering his room

Skip it -> Magical Ticket Glitch to ticket out

To Barlowe

Argila Swamp

Mystery Manor

Albus Fight One

Open the fight by dashing to Albus

3 Anger Casts -> Puff -> Dash behind him as he runs away -> 3 Anger Casts -> Grando + Macir Union Cancel until he's dead

If you mess up the Anger Casts, you might need to use some regular Vol Hasta attacks to finish him

I think you can fit in an extra Puff Union as well if needed after the second set of Angers

Bad Ending Trigger

Absorb Dominus Agony, skip the Barlowe Cutscene, then Magical Ticket Glitch out

Regenerate your hearts and heal if needed, most likely needed unless u want to go slow in Manor

Make you way back to Albus' Boss Room -> Skip the Cutscene -> Ticket Out -> Go to Barlowe's Room -> Equip All Three Dominus Pieces then Union to trigger Bad Ending

Skip the cutscene then watch the Bad Ending

Albus Fight Two

Requip Double Anger then dash into the room and to Albus and do the Triple Anger Casts -> Puff Union

Dash behind him again as he runs away, do a Hasta Union then start finishing him off with regular Vol Hasta hits

If he jumps away, follow him to keep up the damage

You can also bait his Flame Kicks to him in place

Do not go below 75 Hearts

Barlowe Fight

Equip Dominus Agony on your Macir + Grando Sleeve

Grando + Macir Union Cancel with Dominus Agony on and kill Barlowe

You should have just enough hearts to kil him

Before ticketing out, I recommend replacing Agony with Magnes to make killing Cats later easier

Regen to about 30/40 then heal and leave for the Castle. You can skip this heal if you're willing to take a risk and wait for the Castle entrance save

Time for RNG

Castle

Cat Segment

Skip the cutscene and dash through the entrance, making sure to conserve your MP

Divekick through the hallway enemies, don't get poisoned. Divekicking over Ghouls follows the same principles as the Chosen Unes

In the first big room, we encounter Arma Felix, a crucial enemy for this run as we need its Glyph Drop

Use Vol Grando and Macir regular attacks to clear out the Peeping Eyes and Fleamen, and Grando + Macir Union Cancel to kill the Cats here

Ideally, you get the drop in this room, but it's a 4.7% chance individually and a 41% chance across the total we kill, according to Halgorithm

I will say, when I got my PB, it was more uncommon for me to straight up never get one

Grab the Heart Max Up before leaving the big room, and in the next vertical room with the Blood Skeletons, grab the Tasty Meat

Continue to Wallman, killing any Felixes on the way if you have not gotten the drop yet

Some people grind the drop in the HP Max Up, DO NOT DO THIS. Grinding early when you still have 3 kills on the way is not optimal

Also some useful tech before Wallman's room is to divekick off the Fomor to get up to the boss door

Kill Wallman, then replace your Anger Sleeve with Double Vol Hasta + Paries

There are 2 more cats after Wallman, and if you don't end up getting one, you can grind the last one next to the Peeping Eye (or just reset honestly)

Nitesco

After the last cat, we want to take the right path to Forsaken Cloister

Dash/Damage boost through, using the Fomors for i-frames

Grab the Warp to the left of the area exit before continuing right to Cloister

If you have spare hearts, kill the Cave Trolls with Macir + Grando Union otherwise
Slide under the first and Vol Hasta the next

Absorb Nitesco from the first Nova Skeleton, then Magical Ticket out

Regenerate your hearts, save and reset

Final Boss Rush

Go to Course 1 then Magical Ticket Glitch out

Dash to the save Equipment Menu -> Replace Vol Grando with Nitesco and add Dominus Agony -> Replace Double Hasta with Nitesco and add Arma Felix -> Save -> Reset

Go to Course 2, you should now be Albus

Blackmore

Go through the portal

A quirk with this glitched state is we can use Felix by pressing X and Nitesco with R, buttons might be swapped depending on controller

Jump in -> Optical Shot -> Nitesco -> Land -> Flame Kick Right and Get Hit -> Optical Shot -> Nitesco -> Go Under During Phase Transtion -> Neutral Flame Kick

Try for this sequence of attacks and adapt based on his pattern, ideally he should not slam or lunge forward

The fight shouldn't be longer than 15 seconds, ideally under 10

Do not go below 90 Hearts

Eligor

High jump -> Teleport onto His Back -> Press X to transform into Felix -> Untransform to become Shanoa

Turn on Agony -> 4x Nitesco + Macir Union Cancels -> When Dead, Turn off Agony -> Backdash Off -> Dive Kick to Portal

When teleporting onto Eligor, teleport to the space between his sword and body then to his back

Death

Turn on Agony -> 2x Nitesco + Macir Union Cancels -> Backdash Away -> Replace Arma Felix with Agony on Double Nitesco Page

Heal to 400 HP or Greater -> Start Killing Death with Regular Nitesco Lasers + Agony -> When Dead Replace Agony with Magnes -> Magical Ticket Glitch out

You currently have Moonwalkers equipped, so use that to backdash through any unavoidable attacks like his Scythe Trap

If you got a Heart Refresh at the start of the game, use it after those first 2 Unions during the heal menu, then use the extra hearts for Unions

To Volaticus

Take the heal at Village -> Go to Castle -> Warp to Forsaken Cloister's Entrance

With Moonwalkers our Magnes Dashes will be faster and have i-frames, so use it to dash through enemies till the Cerberus Gate

The Boss Rush glitch opened the gate for us, so we can just ascend it. Make sure to grab the Eisbein at the top

Enter Final Approach, since we're still in boss rush we can take a lot more hits on our way to Volaticus so be liberal with the Damage Boosts

Kill Imps if they're in you're way as their debuff will cost you a lot of time, use Nitesco on the Ruler Swords to not get hit

In the second room, you can sometimes divekick off the Imps then kick the Lizardman to get over them, otherwise boost off the Imps

Continue left by sliding under the Cave Troll, dive kicking over the Succubus, and boosting throughFinal Knights

Fall down to Volaticus, absorb it, and equip it on your Double Hasta Page

To Dracula

Fly up to the ceiling room, use double Hasta to kill the Bugbears and grab the MP Max Up in the rightmost Red Chest

Leave and fly right to the double Final Knight room and dash through them again. You should use your Eisbein here

In the Cavetroll room, slide under the first one,

One the second level of this room, do a Jump Union to kill the next 2 above you and freely dash to the next room

Boost through the Devil and Final Knights, use Unions to kill the 2 Devils before the big room

Grab Judgment Ring in the breakable wall to the right in the final big room, then make your way left while killing the Devil with your last few hearts

Grab the Heart Max Up -> divekick off the Bugbear -> Dash through the Devil and Skeleton Blaze then fly up to the save before Dracula

If you need to kill the enemies after the Heart Max Up, use normal Double Nitesco attacks

Finally, save before Dracula and reset to load the main save

Dracula

On the Double Nitesco equip page, equip Dominus Agony

Equip all three Dominus Pieces on the Volaticus page then equip Strength Ring and Judgement Ring

Enter the fight -> skip the cutscene ->Agony and Nitesco + Macir Union Cancel until 0 Hearts -> Agony and Regular Nitesco Lasers until he dies

Switch to the Dominus Page and end the run with the Union

Congratulations, you finished your first NMC run!

Also, I like going to the left of Dracula to do my Unions, but only because it's easier for me to cancel in that direction
